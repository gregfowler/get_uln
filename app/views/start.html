Before you start

ULNs may only be be created for residents of the United Kingdom (UK).

You may have a ULN already if one of the following are true:

  • you are under 31 years old and went to a state school in England
  • you are under 29 years old and went to a state school in Wales
  • you are under 26 years old and went to a state school in Northern Ireland
  • you have undertaken training at a college or provider that was funded by the Education and Skills Funding Agency more recently than 2007

You can also ask your previous school or college for your ULN, provided they still hold your records.
